FAQ



What do I press to go to the home screen?

You press the SFL logo in the corner, and that will automaticly take you to the home page.


How do I know who made a document?

Below the document title is the name of the creator, and it should be in the document too!


How can I suggest a document or a whole subject?

You can email a member of SFL, and they will meet up with the team to consider what you asked for. Their emails are in the "About/Contact" section.


What does SFL mean?

It is an acronym that stands for "Science for Louisville".


Why does this website close sometimes on Saturdays or Sundays?

Every Saturday or Sunday at a random time, the website will close for 5-20 minutes so it can update and add new articles.
